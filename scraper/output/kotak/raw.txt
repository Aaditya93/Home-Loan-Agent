Prepayment Charges
Prepayment/foreclosure is NIL for floating rate loans for non-business end-use.
No prepayment/foreclosure is allowed till the lapse of the lock-in period of 6 months after home loan EMI commencement.
After the lapse of the lock-in period, part prepayment of a minimum Rs 25,000 and a maximum of up to 25% of the outstanding loan amount can be made without any prepayment charges every 6 months.
The minimum period between any two prepayments shall be at least 6 months for part prepayment made over 25% of the outstanding loan amount.
2% of the foreclosure loan amount along with amounts prepaid during the last 12 months shall be charged.
