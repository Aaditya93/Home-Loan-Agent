The following documents are required for a home loan application with Kotak Mahindra Bank:
- Duly signed application form with a photograph
- PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport
- Address Proof
- Bank statements
- Salary slip for the last 3 months (for salaried applicants)
- ITR and Form 16 (for salaried applicants)
- Job Confirmation Proof (for salaried applicants)
- Relationship Proof
- Proof of business existence (for self-employed applicants)
- Business Profile (for self-employed applicants)
- Business Reference (for self-employed applicants)
- Power of Attorney (if applicable)
- Information regarding loans availed with its track record
- Education qualification certificate and proof of business existence
- ITR for the last 3 years with income computation (for self-employed applicants)
- Balance sheet for last 3 years along with profit and loss account certified by a Chartered Accountant (for self-employed applicants)
- All property documents with sanction map
