# HDFC Bank — Interest Rates & FAQs

Here is a summary of HDFC Bank Home Loan interest rates, fees, tenure options, and commonly asked questions:

-   **Interest Rates:**
    -   Starting from 7.90% p.a. onwards.
    -   Special rates available from 7.20% p.a. onwards when applied through Paisabazaar, depending on credit profile.
    -   HDFC Bank uses the repo rate as the benchmark for setting housing loan interest rates.
    -   Applicants with credit scores of 750 and above generally receive lower interest rates.
    -   The 0.05% interest rate concession for women home loan applicants has been discontinued.

-   **Loan Amount:**
    -   Ranges from ₹15 Lakhs up to ₹10 Crores for regular home loans.

-   **Loan to Value (LTV) Ratio (of Property/Renovation Cost):**
    -   **HDFC Bank Housing Loans & Balance Transfer:**
        -   Up to and including ₹30 Lakhs: Up to 90%
        -   ₹30.01 Lakhs - ₹75 Lakhs: Up to 80%
        -   Above ₹75 Lakhs: Up to 75%
    -   **HDFC Bank Rural Housing Loans, Plot Loans, Home Extension Loans, Home Renovation Loans (for New Customers):**
        -   Up to and including ₹30 Lakhs: Up to 80% (90% for Extension/Renovation New Customers)
        -   ₹30.01 Lakhs - ₹75 Lakhs: Up to 80%
        -   Above ₹75 Lakhs: Up to 75%
    -   **HDFC Home Renovation Loans (for Existing Customers):**
        -   100% of renovation cost across all loan amount slabs.

-   **Tenure Options:**
    -   **Up to 30 years:** Regular Housing Loan, Home Loan Balance Transfer, Rural Housing Loans.
    -   **Up to 20 years:** Home Extension Loans.
    -   **Up to 15 years:** Plot Loans, Home Renovation Loans.

-   **Processing Fees (Exclusive of GST):**
    -   **Resident Housing Loan/Extension/House Renovation Loan/Refinance of Housing Loan/Plot Loans for Housing:**
        -   For Salaried: ₹3,500
        -   For Self-employed Professionals: ₹10,000 or 0.25% of the loan amount, whichever is greater.
    -   **Resident Housing/Extension/Renovation/Refinance/Plot Loans for Self-Employed Non-Professionals:**
        -   Up to 1.50% of the loan amount or ₹5,000, whichever is higher.
        -   Minimum Retention Amount: 50% of applicable fees or ₹5,000.
    -   **HDFC Home Loan for NRI:**
        -   Up to 1.50% of the loan amount or ₹3,300, whichever is higher.
        -   Minimum Retention Amount: 50% of applicable fees or ₹3,300.
    -   **Value Plus Loans:**
        -   Up to 1.50% of the loan amount or ₹5,000.
        -   Minimum Retention Amount: 50% of applicable fees or ₹5,000.
    -   **Re-Appraisal of loan after 6 months from sanction:**
        -   Salaried & Self-employed Professionals: Up to ₹3,300.
        -   Self-Employed Non-Professionals/NRI/Value Plus Loans/HDFC Reach Scheme: Up to ₹5,000.

-   **Other Charges:**
    -   **Stamp Duty/MOD/MOE/Registration:** As applicable in respective states. MOD charges typically range from 0.1% to 0.5% of the loan amount.
    -   **CERSAI Charges:** As per actuals.
    -   **Charges levied by third parties (e.g., mortgage guarantee company):** Up to 18% p.a. on overdue instalment amounts.

-   **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):**
    -   **Prepayment:** Partial or full prepayment is allowed. No prepayment charges apply to home loans availed at floating interest rates. For fixed-rate home loans, a prepayment fee of 2% of the loan amount plus applicable taxes is charged, unless pre-closure is made through own sources.
    -   **Interest Certificate:** Available via the official HDFC Bank website, HDFC Bank Home Loan mobile app, or by visiting a bank branch.
    -   **EMI Calculation:** Can be calculated using the HDFC Home Loan EMI Calculator by inputting the loan amount, tenure, and interest rate.
    -   **Co-applicants:** Co-owners of the property must be co-applicants. Not all co-applicants need to be co-owners; close family members are usually allowed. A spouse's income can be considered to enhance loan eligibility.
    -   **Interest Rate Reduction for Existing Borrowers:** Existing borrowers can request a reduction. If approved, a conversion fee of up to 0.50% of the outstanding principal and undisbursed amount (if any) or ₹3,000, whichever is lower, will apply. If the bank does not reduce rates, borrowers can consider transferring their loan to another lender.
    -   **Pre-approved Home Loan Facility:** An in-principle approval based on financial position, creditworthiness, and income. Property must be purchased within 6 months from the date of loan sanction.
    -   **Loan Disbursal:** Occurs after property and document verification and completion of the 'own contribution' or down payment. Disbursal can be requested online or offline and can be complete or in stages depending on construction progress.
    -   **Repayment Modes:** Includes Electronic Clearing System (ECS), direct deduction of monthly instalments by the employer, or issuing post-dated cheques from the salary account.
