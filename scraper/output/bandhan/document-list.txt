# Bandhan Bank — Document Requirements

The following documents are generally required for a Bandhan Bank Home Loan application:

**For Salaried Borrowers (Suraksha/Sajavat/Suvidha Home Loan):**
- Completely filled loan application form with a recent photograph.
- Photo ID proof, address proof, age proof for KYC.
- Completely filled customer relationship form with recent photograph.
- Cheque for applicable processing charges.
- Salary slips for the last 3 months.
- Recent PF statement.
- Form 16 provided by the employer.
- Last 3 years ITR copies.
- Photocopy of investments and life insurance policies.
- Copy of bank passbook for all bank accounts showing entries for the last 3 months.
- Credit card photocopy with last monthly statement.
- **If other loans are availed (open or closed):**
  - Sanction letter copy.
  - No dues certificate for closed loans.
  - Updated repayment history.
- Salary certificate on employer’s letterhead with signatures and stamp (if no salary slips).
- Copy of rent agreement if present accommodation is on rent.
- Visiting card.

**For Self-employed Borrowers (Suraksha/Sajavat/Suvidha Home Loan):**
- Completely filled loan application form with a recent photograph.
- KYC documents.
- Customer Relationship Form.
- Cheque for applicable processing charges.
- Copy of ITR for past 3 years.
- Copy of certified accounts or audited accounts with schedules for the last three financial years.
- Business note (in bank format) with images of business place.
- Investment and life insurance policies photocopy.
- Copy of bank passbooks of all bank accounts for the last 6 months.
- Rent agreement of the place of business, if on rent.
- Shop and establishment license for the commercial entity.
- Copy of professional degree/certificate.
- Visiting card.
- Shareholding pattern or partnership deed of your entity.

**Additional Legal and Technical Documents (Common for Suraksha/Sajavat/Suvidha Home Loan):**
- Approved construction plan with estimate, construction permission, and layout plan.
- Ownership document copy of the previous owner.
- Copy of sale deed/construction agreement/sell agreement/amenities agreement.
- Recent images of the property.
- Ownership proof in revenue records (e.g., Form-6, property card, Form 8-A, Form 7/12).
- Receipts of margin money paid.
- Copy of allotment letter and share certificate in case of co-operative society.
- NA permission from the concerned authority.
- NOC to mortgage from a concerned authority (e.g., development authority/society).

**For Salaried Borrowers (Su-awas Home Loan):**
- Filled application form.
- Photo ID proof, age proof, address proof for KYC.
- Last 3 months salary slips.
- Form 16.
- Copy of bank passbook for all bank accounts showing entries for the last 6 months.
- **In case of other loans (active as well as closed):**
  - Sanction letter copy.
  - Updated repayment record.
  - No dues for closed loans.
- Salary certificate (in case of no salary slips).
- Copy of rent agreement.

**For Self-employed Borrowers (Su-awas Home Loan):**
- Application form.
- KYC documents.
- **Any one of the following for income proof:**
  - Cash flow statement and last 1 year’s ITR statement OR verification by local authorities (Municipality or Panchayat).
  - ITR for the last 2 years with income computation for both years (if eligibility is calculated based on ITR income).
  - Self-declared income statement as per the Bank’s format (if the monthly income of the self-employed applicant is less than or equal to ₹25,000).
- Copy of bank passbook with entries of last 6 months for all banks.
- **In case of other loans availed (active as well as closed):**
  - Copy of sanction letter.
  - No dues of closed loans.
  - Updated repayment record.
- Rent agreement of the business place.
- Rent agreement copy of accommodation.

**Additional Legal and Technical Documents (Common for Su-awas Home Loan):**
- Non-agricultural permission from the authority concerned.
- Ownership document of the previous owner.
- Latest image of the property.
- Copy of agreement to sell/sale deed/amenities agreement/construction agreement.
- Ownership proof in revenue records (e.g., Form 8-A, Form 7/12, Form 6, property card).
- Receipt of margin money paid (in case of property purchase).
- Approved construction plan with layout plan, construction permission, and estimate.
