# Bandhan Bank — Interest Rates & FAQs

Here is a summary of Bandhan Bank's home loan interest rates, fees, tenure options, and frequently asked questions:

**Interest Rates:**
- General range: 8.41% to 15.00% p.a. onwards.
- Specific to loan types:
  - Suraksha Home Loan: 8.41%-12.58% p.a.
  - Sajavat Home Loan: 8.41%-12.58% p.a.
  - Su-awas Home Loan: 13.00%-15.00% p.a.
  - Suvidha Home Loan: 8.41%-12.58% p.a.
- The final interest rate may depend on factors such as monthly income, job profile, employer’s profile, and credit score of the applicant.

**Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio & Loan Amounts:**
- Up to 90% of the property cost for Suraksha and Suvidha Home Loans.
- Up to 80% of the cost of repair work for Sajavat Home Loan.
- Su-awas Home Loan: Loan amount ranges from ₹1 Lakh to ₹10 Lakh.
- Su-awas Saral Home Loan: Loan amount ranges from ₹1 Lakh to ₹7 Lakh.
- Suniwas Home Loan: Loan amount ranges from ₹50,000 to ₹5 Lakh.

**Tenure Options:**
- General range: 1 to 30 years.
- Specific to loan types:
  - Suraksha Home Loan: 1-30 years.
  - Sajavat Home Loan: 1-15 years.
  - Su-awas Home Loan: 2-10 years.
  - Su-awas Saral Home Loan: 2-10 years.
  - Suvidha Home Loan: 1-30 years.

**Fees and Charges:**
- Processing Fees: Up to 1%.
- Login Fees: ₹3,500 + GST.
- Administrative Fees: 0.25% – 2.00% + GST.
- **Pre-payment Charges on Fixed Rate of Interest:**
  - For loans repaid less than 12 months: 4% of outstanding principal + GST.
  - For loans repaid more than 12 months: 2% of outstanding principal + GST.
  - For cases disbursed under Erstwhile Gruh (with documentary proof of own source of funds): NIL.
- **Pre-payment Charges on Floating Rate of Interest:**
  - If the applicant and co-applicant are individuals: NIL.
  - For one or more non-individual applicants: 2% + GST.
- Rate of Interest Conversion Charges: 0.50%-1.25% of the outstanding balance (for PLR linked loans); ₹3,000 for EBR conversion.
- Charges for Cheque Bounce: ₹500 + GST.
- Penal Charges (late payment of EMIs): 2% of the overdue amount.
- Account Statement: Free once annually; ₹100 + GST for subsequent requests.
- Income Tax Certificate: Free once annually; ₹30 + GST for subsequent requests.
- CERSAI Charges: For creation: ₹100 + GST; For satisfaction: NIL.
- CIC Charges: ₹150 per individual + GST.
- Valuation Report Charges: As per actuals, but not exceeding ₹5,000 per property.
- Technical Inspection Charges (for subsequent inspections during phase-wise disbursement):
  - For local visits: ₹250 per visit + GST.
  - For outstation visits: ₹350 per visit + GST.
- Search Report and TCC Charges: Maximum ₹5,000 per property.
- Charges for registration of mortgage and release of charges: On actuals.
- Copy of Property Documents: ₹500 + GST.
- Stamp duty and Statutory Charges: At actuals as per applicable state laws.
- Miscellaneous/Legal Charges: At actuals.
- List of Documents: ₹250.

**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):**
- **What is the current home loan interest rate offered by Bandhan Bank?** The current home loan interest rates offered by Bandhan Bank range from 8.41% to 15% p.a., depending on the credit profile of applicants and the specific home loan scheme availed.
- **How can I apply for a Bandhan Bank Home Loan?** You can apply either directly through the official website of Bandhan Bank or by visiting any branch.
- **What factors determine Bandhan Bank Home Loan eligibility?** Eligibility primarily depends on the monthly disposable or surplus income of home loan applicants.
- **What is the minimum credit score required to avail home loans from Bandhan Bank?** Bandhan Bank has not specified a cut-off credit score. However, applicants with credit scores of 750 and above generally have higher chances of availing home loans at lower interest rates.
- **Can I prepay my home loan availed from Bandhan Bank?** Yes, you can prepay the home loan. The bank does not levy any prepayment charges on home loans offered at floating rates of interest if the applicant and co-applicant are individuals.
- **Can I choose between fixed and floating rates of interest?** Bandhan Bank offers home loans at floating rates only.
- **I do not have any formal income documents. Can I avail home loan from Bandhan Bank?** Yes, if you do not have formal income proofs like salary slips with PF deductions and IT returns, you can avail Suvidha Home Loans for purchase/construction/extension of a house. The bank conducts detailed field investigations and assessments to assess cash flow and income for such applicants.
- **Can micro-banking borrowers avail home loans from Bandhan Bank?** Yes, Bandhan Bank offers the Su-awas Home Loan scheme specifically to micro-banking borrowers.
