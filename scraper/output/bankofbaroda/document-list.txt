# Bank of Baroda — Document Requirements

The following documents are generally required for a Bank of Baroda Home Loan application:

- **Common Documents for all Applicants:**
  - Duly filled application form with applicant's photograph.
  - Proof of Residence (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Voter’s ID Card, Passport, Driving License, Ration Card).
  - Proof of Age (e.g., PAN card, Aadhaar Card, Voter’s ID Card, Birth Certificate, Passport, Driving License).
  - Proof of assets held (e.g., LIC, NSC, KVP, MF, Property).
  - Asset and liability statement.
  - ITR verification report.

- **Additional Documents for Salaried Individuals:**
  - Latest 3 months’ salary slip (and 1 month salary slip for a guarantor).
  - Copy of employee ID card issued by the employer.
  - Past 1-year ITR and Form 16 of applicant and guarantor.
  - Bank account statement for the past 6 months (or Bank account number if with Bank of Baroda).
  - Appointment letter, confirmation letter, promotion letter, or increment letter evidencing duration of employment.

- **Additional Documents for Self-employed Individuals/Professionals/Others:**
  - Balance sheet and profit and loss account with income computation for last 2 years.
  - ITR for last 2 years, 26AS, Traces.
  - IT assessment, clearance certificate, Income Tax challan, TDS certificate (Form 16A), Form 26AS for income declared in ITR.
  - Business Proof (e.g., Gumasta license, Service Tax Registration, Registration Certificate).

- **For Applicants engaged in Business through Partnership Firm/Private Ltd. Company (additional to self-employed):**
  - Address Proof or PAN Card of Firm/Company.
  - ITR and audited results of last 2 years of Firm or Company.
  - Memorandum and Articles of Association (AOA) of the company.
  - Current account statement for last 1 year.

- **Additional Documents for Farmers or Agriculturists:**
  - Talati’s/Gram Sevak/Village Revenue Officer Certificate for income of the past 2 years.
  - Mamladaar’s/Block Revenue Officer certificate for income of the past 2 years.
  - Bank account statement for the past 12 months (individual).
  - Land revenue records (Form 6, 7/12, 8A).

- **Additional Documents for NRI/PIO/OCI:**
  - Duly filled application form with signatures and photographs.
  - Copy of passport with VISA stamped.
  - Details of local contact person (name, address, relation, contact numbers).
  - Residence Proof (India and abroad, e.g., Driver’s license, ration card, voter ID, passport, Aadhaar card, registered rent agreement).
  - Overseas bank account statement for the last 6 months.
  - NRE savings bank statement of account for the last 6 months.
  - Account statement for all existing loans for the last 1 year.
  - Credit check reports from Overseas Credit Bureau.
  - **For PIO (additional):**
    - Current passport showing birthplace in India or abroad.
    - Indian passport, if held earlier.
    - Passport of parents or grandparents substantiating PIO claim.
  - **For Salaried Individuals (NRI/PIO/OCI):**
    - Employment contract copy in vernacular language.
    - Copy of ID card issued by the current employer.
    - Copy of ITR of the individual for the last 2 years (India/abroad).
    - Latest salary slips for the last 6 months.
    - Continuous discharge certificate, if applicable.
    - Copy of latest work permit.
  - **For Self-employed Individuals (NRI/PIO/OCI):**
    - Balance sheet and profit and loss account of business or profession.
    - Copies of individual’s ITR for the last 3 years (India/abroad).
    - Continuous discharge certificate, if applicable.
    - Business proof, place of business, and address proof.
    - A note providing information on the nature of business or profession.
