# State Bank of India — Document Requirements

The following documents are generally required for an SBI Home Loan:

- **General Documents:**
  - Duly filled application form with 3 passport-sized photographs
  - Employer ID card
  - ID Proof: PAN Card/Voter ID/Passport/Driving License
  - Address Proof: Copy of telephone bill/electricity bill/water bill/piped gas bill/passport copy/Aadhar card

- **General Documents for SBI NRI Home Loan:**
  - Employer Identity Card
  - Attested copy of valid Passport and visa
  - Address proof of current overseas address
  - Loan application form duly filled with 3 Passport size photographs
  - For Merchant Navy applicants: Copy of Continuous Discharge Certificate (CDC)
  - For Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs): PIO Card issued by the Government of India
  - Document attestation may be done by Indian Embassy/Consulate, FOs/Rep., Overseas Notary Public, or officials of Branch/Sourcing outfits based in India.

- **Property Papers:**
  - Permission for construction
  - Registered Sale agreement (mandatory for Maharashtra), Allotment letter, Stamped Sale Agreement
  - Occupancy certificate (for ready-to-move-in properties)
  - Maintenance bill, electricity bill, property tax receipt
  - Copy of approved plan and Registered Development Agreement of the builder, Conveyance Deed (for New Property)
  - Bank account details or payment receipts for payments made to the builder

- **Account Statements:**
  - Bank account statement for the last 6 months for all bank accounts
  - Loan account statement for the last 1 year for previous loans from other financial institutions
  - For NRIs: Bank account details for the last 6 months overseas account showing salary, savings, and Indian account (if any).

- **Income Proof - Salaried Applicant/Co-applicant/Guarantor:**
  - Last 3 months salary slip/certificate
  - Copy of ITR/Form 16, acknowledged by IT Department
  - Bank Statement of last 6 months showing salary credit
  - **For NRIs (additional):**
    - Valid work permit
    - Copy of last year’s Individual Tax Return (except for NRIs/PIOs in Middle East countries and Merchant Navy employees)
    - Employment contract (English translation duly attested by employer or consulate or foreign office or Embassy, if in another language)
    - Latest original salary certificate/slip and copy of identity card issued by current employer

- **Income Proof - Non-Salaried Applicant/Co-applicant/Guarantor:**
  - ITR for the last 3 years
  - Business Address Proof
  - Profit and loss account and balance sheet for last 3 years
  - TDS Certificate (Form 16A)
  - Qualification Certificate (for CA, Doctors, and other professionals)
  - Business License Details
  - Individual Tax Return of last 2 years (except for NRI/PIO in Middle East countries)
  - Bank Statement of overseas account of last 6 months in the name of individual as well as company/unit
  - Proof of business address
  - Proof of income in case of self-employed professionals/businessmen
  - Audited/C.A. certified Balance Sheet and P&L accounts of last 2 years
