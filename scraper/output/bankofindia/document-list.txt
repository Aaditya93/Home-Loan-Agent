The following documents are required for a Bank of India Home Loan:

For Individuals (General / BOI Star Diamond Home Loan):
-   **Identity Proof:** Passport, PAN Card, Driving License, or Voter ID.
-   **Address Proof:** Passport, Driving License, Aadhaar Card, latest electricity bill, latest telephone bill, or latest piped gas bill.
-   **Income Proof (Salaried):** Salary/pay slip for the latest 6 months and 1 year ITR/Form 16.
-   **Income Proof (Self-employed):** ITR for the last 3 years along with income computation of Income, Profit & Loss account, Balance sheet, and Capital account statement.

For Other than Individuals (Firms/Companies - General / BOI Star Diamond Home Loan):
-   KYC of partners/directors.
-   PAN card copy of the firm/company.
-   Account statement for the last 1 year.
-   Registered Partnership Deed/Memorandum of Association (MOA)/Articles of Association (AOA).
-   Audited financials of the firm for the last 3 years.
-   Certificate of incorporation.

Additional Documents for BOI Star Pravasi Home Loan (For NRIs/PIO Individuals):
-   Passport with VISA printed on it.
-   Copy of PAN Card.
-   Work permit.
-   Address Proof of India and abroad.
-   ID card issued by the employer.
-   Address Proof of the employer with contact details.
-   Latest salary slip.
-   Annual ITR filed as per the rules of the country of present residence for the last 2 years.
-   Statement/Income Proof/Returns, etc., as applicable to the country of present residence.

Additional Documents for BOI Star Pravasi Home Loan (For NRIs/PIO Other than Individuals/Firms):
-   Membership of professional organization.
-   Income Proof (based on audited financial).
-   Copy of PAN Card.
-   Passport with VISA printed on it.
-   Address Proof of India and abroad.
-   Government or local body permission or license to engage in business activity.
