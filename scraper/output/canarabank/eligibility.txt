# Canara Bank — Eligibility Criteria

Canara Bank offers various home loan schemes with specific eligibility criteria:

For Canara Bank Housing Loan (General):
- Salaried individuals: Minimum 2 years of continuous service, with at least 6 months in the current organization.
- Self-employed, professionals, or individuals engaged in business activities: Must have been in their profession or business for at least 3 years.
- Minimum age: Less than 60 years.
- Maximum age: Up to 75 years.

For Canara Bank Housing Loan to Agriculturists:
- Agriculturists owning and cultivating agricultural land of more than 5 acres.
- Agriculturists engaged in allied activities (like plantation, horticulture, dairy, poultry) with a minimum gross annual income of ₹5 Lakh.

For Canara Bank NRI Home Loan:
- NRIs with a valid Indian passport or Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) with a foreign passport.
- Minimum age: 21 years.
- Maximum age: 60 years.
- NRI status: Maintained for 3 years.
- Minimum work experience abroad: 2 years.

For Canara Home Loan Super Gain:
- Available to new and existing home loan borrowers availing home loans of ₹20 Lakh and above.

For Canara Home Loan Plus:
- Existing home loan borrowers of Canara Bank with a satisfactory repayment track record.
- Minimum age: 18 years.
- Maximum age: 70 years.

For Canara Kuteer:
- Individuals residing in rural, urban, and metro cities, and members of self-help groups.
- Household income: Up to ₹1 Lakh per annum, or between ₹1 Lakh to ₹3 Lakh per annum.

Credit Score:
- Canara Bank has not specified a minimum cut-off CIBIL score. However, the bank considers the credit score and other credit profile aspects (monthly income, occupation, employer's profile) to assign credit risk grades (CRG). Applicants with a lower credit risk grade may be offered lower interest rates.
