# Canara Bank — Interest Rates & FAQs

Canara Bank Home Loans come with the following details regarding interest rates, fees, tenure, and common questions:

Interest Rates:
- Starting from 7.15% p.a. onwards.
- Interest rates are based on the loan amount, credit score, applicant type, and the specific home loan scheme opted for.
- A 5 bps (basis points) interest rate concession is offered for:
  - Takeover or Ready-to-move-in home loan proposals.
  - Salaried employees working with Central/State Government, PSUs, MNCs, Bluechip Companies, or other Corporates who maintain or switch their salary account to Canara Bank, including those under the Premium Payroll Package.
- An additional 5 bps interest rate concession is offered for women applicants.
- Canara Bank offers home loans at both floating and fixed interest rates.

Processing Charges:
- NIL (Zero processing charges for Canara Bank Home Loans).

Loan Tenure Options:
- General Home Loans: Up to 30 years.
- Canara Housing Loans (all variants): Up to 30 years or up to 75 years of age, whichever is earlier.
- Canara Home Loan Plus: Up to 15 years or the remaining repayment period of the existing home loan.
- Canara Site Loan: Up to 10 years, or the maximum period for house construction, or up to 65 years of age of the borrowers.
- Canara Bank Home Loan for NRIs: Up to 30 years or 60 years of age of the borrower (extendable to 70 years under certain conditions).

Loan Amount (Maximum):
- Canara Housing Loan (Salaried): Up to 72 times last drawn monthly gross salary (or up to 96 times for select cases).
- Canara Housing Loan (Self-employed): Up to 6 times of average gross annual income of the last 3 years (or up to 8 years for select cases).
- For repair/renovation: Up to ₹15 Lakh.
- For furnishing and fixed enhancement: Up to 25% of the loan amount or ₹50 Lakh, whichever is lower.
- Canara Home Loan Plus: Up to ₹50 Lakh (24 months gross salary for salaried; twice the average gross income for 3 years for non-salaried).
- Canara Kuteer: Up to ₹5 Lakh for household income up to ₹1 Lakh; Up to ₹10 Lakh for household income ₹1 Lakh-₹3 Lakh.
- Canara Site Loan: 75% of the plot's cost/guideline value or 3 years gross annual salary/income.
- Canara Home Loan Super Gain: ₹20 Lakh onwards.
- Canara Bank Home Loan for NRIs: 4 times of annual gross income (or 5 years of gross income in select cases).

Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio:
- For loan amounts up to ₹30 Lakh:
  - New house/flat or old house/flat (up to 10 years old): Up to 90%.
  - Old house/flat (more than 10 years old): Up to 75%.
- For loan amounts between ₹30 Lakh to ₹75 Lakh:
  - New house/flat or old house/flat (up to 10 years old): Up to 80%.
- For loan amounts above ₹75 Lakh:
  - New house/flat or old house/flat (up to 10 years old): Up to 75%.
- For repair/renovation/upgradation/expansion: Up to 75% of the project cost.

Prepayment and Foreclosure:
- No prepayment charges are levied on home loans at floating rates of interest.

Repayment Commencement:
- For purchasing an already built house/flat: Repayment starts within 2 months from the date of loan disbursement.
- For plot purchase and house construction thereon, or only construction of a house on an existing site: Repayment starts within 2 months after house construction or 2 years from the date of the first disbursement, whichever is earlier.

Plot Purchase Restriction:
- Housing loan proceeds cannot be used *solely* for plot purchases. In cases where the loan is for both plot purchase and house construction, borrowers cannot utilize beyond 60% of the loan proceeds/sanctioned loan amount for plot purchase.

Other Benefits:
- Applicants can avail Canara Bank Home Loans for purchase or construction of a house/flat, repair/renovation of existing property, and takeover of home loans.
- All home loan borrowers are eligible for life insurance cover.
- The bank offers a balance transfer facility to existing home loan borrowers of other lenders.

Application Process:
- Apply online via the official Canara Bank website or the Canara ai1 mobile app.
- Apply offline by visiting any Canara Bank branch.
