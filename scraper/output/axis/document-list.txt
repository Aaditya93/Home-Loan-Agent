# Axis Bank — Document Requirements

The following documents are required for an Axis Bank Home Loan:
- **Application Form**: Duly filled application form.
- **ID Proof**: Aadhaar card, passport, government employee ID, voter ID, driving license, or photo ID issued by the Government of India.
- **Address Proof**: Aadhaar card, passport, government employee ID, voter ID, driving license, photo ID issued by the Government of India, electricity bill, telephone bill, gas bill, or property tax receipt.
- **Date of Birth Proof**: Passport, PAN Card, Aadhaar card, Birth certificate, or SSC Marksheet.
- **Signature Proof**: Passport, PAN Card, Banker’s verification, or Notarized affidavit with address and ID proof.

**Income Proof for Salaried Individuals**:
- Pay slip for 3 months.
- 2 years bonus proof or pay slip for 6 months.
- Bank statement for 6 months reflecting salary credits.
- Form 16 for 2 years.

**Income Proof for NRI (Salaried)**:
- Pay slip for 3 months.
- Contract letter/appointment letter.
- Passport Copy.
- Valid VISA copy.
- Overseas credit report.
- Continuous Discharge Certificate for shipping cases.
- International salary account statement for 6 months.
- Domestic/NRE/NRO account statement for 6 months.

**Income Proof for Self-Employed Individuals**:
- ITR, income computation, P&L, balance sheet with CA seal and sign for 2 years (For Asha Home Loan, 1 year).
- Bank statement for personal and business accounts for 6 months.
- Business continuity proof for 3 years.
- Tax paid challan and CPC if ITR is filed with no digital signatures.
- Tax Audit Report (if gross turnover exceeds ₹1 crore or gross receipt exceeds ₹25 Lakh).

**For Balance Transfer/Loan Takeover from Other Bank/Financial Institution**:
- Loan account statement for 12 months with a recent outstanding letter.
- Details of current loan and bank statement for 6 months with EMI deduction details.
